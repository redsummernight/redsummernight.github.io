My mind is a cold, barren wasteland.

Ideas flee like startled creatures at the sound of my footsteps.

I envy those who can pack their art with details.
I envy those who know how to use more than three colors.
I envy those with fertile imaginations.

Minimalism is the excuse of the lazy.
